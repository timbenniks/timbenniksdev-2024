#sidea
  :::content-title-block{tag="h3" title="Mini bio"}
  :::

  :::content-richtext
  #body
  Tim Benniks is a Developer Experience and Product leader with over 20 years of experience building digital platforms for some of the world’s biggest brands, including Nike, Google, CHANEL, Louis Vuitton, and Procter & Gamble.

He currently leads Developer Experience at Contentstack, where he also operates as Product Manager across Developer Experience, Developer Hub & Marketplace, Contentstack MCP, and Agent Skills. His work sits at the intersection of product, developers, and emerging AI workflows, with a focus on making complex systems usable, scalable, and actually enjoyable to build with.

Tim is an active voice in the developer community, known for his conference talks, technical content, and collaborations with companies like Cloudinary, Supabase, Algolia, HeyGen, and Nuxt. He serves as the Co-chair of the Enterprise AI Agents Workgroup at the MACH aliance, helping define how modern enterprises adopt composable architectures and AI-driven systems.

Outside of work, he plays guitar and spends time with his family and two Corgis, Princess Nugget and Scarlet.
  :::
